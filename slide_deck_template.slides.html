Investigation Overview¶

This presentation aims to get insights out of the FordGOBike to see what are the most interesting relationships between each variable. what are the facts regarding the duration time and how it's related to another variable such as days or hours or even the type of user?

Dataset Overview¶

The dataset contains data about duration in sec and minutes. start and end station like id or the name , dates in hours , days , months. station geographic like longitude and latitude. bike_id and user_type.
